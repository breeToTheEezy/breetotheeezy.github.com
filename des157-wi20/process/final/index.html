The Causes of Wildfires

  • Burning Debris
  • Unattended Campfires
  • Equipment Failure or Engine Sparks
  • Cigarettes
  • Fireworks
  • Arson

The Effects of Wildfires

Annihilation of peoples’ homes, nature, & wildlife itself. This results in soil that is overheated and never able to be used again due to nutrient deterioration.

Also, if because firefighters use too much water to put out the fires, this causes soil to erode and become unuseable.

Promote Forest Preservation

Remember the acronym: TREES

  1. Teach others about why the environment is important and how to conserve it.
  2. Restore damaged ecosystems by planting trees where forests have been cut down.
  3. Encourage people to live their lives while not harming the environment simultaneously.
  4. Establish parks to protect rainforests and wildlife.
  5. Support companies that help minimize damage to the environment.
