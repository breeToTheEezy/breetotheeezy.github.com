Initial Sketches

What I learned

Being able to brainstorm ideas through sketching helps to give me a few themes I can use to achieve and get a head start in the design process.

Realization #1

Sketching out my ideas helped me to realize that most of my work consists of graphics and even thought it's essentially not a bad thing, it brought to my attention the idea of working with other mediums; other mediums being photography or videos. Since my primary focus is on the natural disaster, wildfires, presenting real life evidence through pictures will be more engaging due to its realism.

Realization #2

In addition, since my overall message I want to convey is to encourage wildfire prevention, it could be useful to create a game to reinforce this. Through the physicality of the game itself, this can implicity enforce action to take place to save the green of the world.
